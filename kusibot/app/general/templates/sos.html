Immediate Help & Crisis Support

If you are going through a difficult time, feeling overwhelmed, or having thoughts of harming yourself, please know that you are not alone and help is available.

Below are some resources available in Spain that offer confidential support. KusiBot cannot provide emergency assistance, please reach out to one of these services.

National Support (Spain)

Línea 024 - Suicide Prevention Hotline
24/7 Available

Official Ministry of Health hotline run by Cruz Roja for anyone experiencing suicidal thoughts or behaviours, or their families/friends. Free, confidential, and anonymous.

Call 024 Website Chat {# Link points to same page where sign language access is mentioned #} Sign Language Access
Teléfono de la Esperanza
24/7 Available

A well-known non-profit organisation offering confidential listening and emotional support for people in crisis situations.

Call 717 003 717 Website Chat

Regional Support (Asturias)

For urgent, life-threatening situations in Asturias, always call 112 or the national 024 line.

For non-emergency mental health support, you can access services through your local primary care center. Find your nearest "Centro de Salud" via AsturSalud.

Remember, reaching out is a sign of strength. There are people who care and want to help.
