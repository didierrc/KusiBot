About KusiBot

Your Friendly Mental Health Companion

KusiBot is an AI-powered chatbot designed to offer a supportive space for discussing mental well-being and provide initial mental health assessments.

This project was developed as a Final Degree Project with the goal of exploring how AI, specifically Multi-Agent Systems (MAS), can be applied in the mental health domain. It aims to serve users seeking a company to talk to in those rough times as well as giving preliminary assessments.

How KusiBot Works

KusiBot operates using a Multi-Agent System architecture:

  • Manager Agent:Orchestrates your input messages routing it to the specific agent.
  • Intent Recogniser Agent:Understands your input using BERT - an AI language model - to determine the intent behind your messages.
  • Conversation Agent:Handles the general flow of conversation, making the interaction feel natural, powered by Mistral 7B (a Large Language Model - LLM).
  • Assessment Agent:Administers standardised mental health questionnaires when a warning intent is detected, currently including the PHQ-9 (for Depression) and GAD-7 (for Anxiety), guiding you through the questions to assess you so other professionals can check out your status.

This approach allows KusiBot to manage different aspects of the conversation effectively, from casual chat to structured assessment.

Important Disclaimer

Not a Substitute for Professional Help

KusiBot is an AI assistant and a student project. It is designed for informational and initial assessment purposes only. It cannot provide a diagnosis and is not a replacement for professional medical advice, psychological therapy, diagnosis, or treatment from a qualified healthcare provider.

If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please do not rely on KusiBot. Contact your local emergency services or a crisis hotline immediately.
